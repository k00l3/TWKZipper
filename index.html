Welcome to TWK Zipper

TWK Zipper East London was established in 1992 and is one of six factories world wide. Our main business comprises of all kind of zippers and sliders manufacturing and zipper dyeing. Zippers from pre-processing and post-processing to dyeing are all produced in-house to ensure quality consistency. Top quality, top service, customer satisfaction and on-time delivery is our promise to our customers.

TTWK is the manufacturer, supplier, of best quality Nylon, Chunky Vislon, Metal, Invisible Zippers and Sliders in South Africa. We of experienced who have made our products that are well accepted by our customers, committed to achieve 100% customer satisfaction. *These are just a few samples of our products, we have a large variety of different zippers and sliders to meet your specific needs.*
